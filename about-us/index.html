    About Vanguard Pharmacy

    Your trusted partner in health and wellness. Serving the Georgetown community with care and dedication.

    Who We Are

    Vanguard Pharmacy is more than just a place to pick up prescriptions. We are a locally owned and operated pharmacy dedicated to providing personalized healthcare services to our community in Georgetown, Ontario.

    Our team of experienced pharmacists and staff are committed to your well-being, offering expert advice, medication management, and a wide range of health services tailored to your needs.
