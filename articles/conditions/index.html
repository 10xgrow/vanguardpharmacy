    Welcome to the Vanguard Pharmacy™ Pharmacy Condition Library. This detailed information resource is designed to support your health and wellness by helping you learn about and better understand medical conditions. All information is regularly reviewed and updated by Ontario doctors and pharmacists. For additional health information, please talk to your Vanguard Pharmacy™ Pharmacist.

    Each Condition Library factsheet contains information about:

    • what causes a disease and how it can be prevented
    • what the symptoms and treatment are
    • what other illnesses can follow
    • and more

    To find out about a condition, just type the name in the box above and click GO. Or search alphabetically - click on a letter for a list of conditions that begin with that letter.
