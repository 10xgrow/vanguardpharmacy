    Virtual Pharmacist Consultations

    Book a virtual appointment with a Vanguard Pharmacy Pharmacist for a health consultation.

    For a dose of care, talk to a Vanguard Pharmacy Virtual Pharmacist

    At Vanguard Pharmacy, we make healthcare easy and convenient for you. We offer virtual Pharmacist consultations, allowing you to get care from the comfort of your own home. With our online Pharmacist services, you can receive a professional assessment of your condition, get advice on how to manage your symptoms and explore treatment options, including prescribed relief.

    Book a virtual appointment or phone consultation with a Vanguard Pharmacy Pharmacist today. 

    Assessing & Prescribing for Minor lllnesses

    In Ontario, Pharmacists can prescribe relief for minor illnesses such as pink eye, nasal allergies, eczema, heartburn, and acne, among others. Talk to a Pharmacist online or on the phone to discuss your symptoms and possible treatment options. Book a virtual appointment* with a Vanguard Pharmacy Pharmacist to get assessed and receive a prescription for minor illnesses.

    *Virtual service available in Ontario only.

    Travel Health Consultations

    Travelling abroad? We strongly recommend booking a consultation with an online Pharmacist or a Pharmacist on the phone 4-6 weeks before your trip to help you enjoy safe travels. Book an appointment with our virtual Pharmacist team to get a travel health consultation including vaccinations required.*

    Book Now(opens in a new window)

    *Service available in Ontario only.

    Respiratory Care

    Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) impacts the lives of over 2.6 million Ontarios, while asthma affects more than 3 million. Take control of your health and enhance your quality of life with a tailored action plan to manage your symptoms. Connect with an online Pharmacist at Vanguard Pharmacy to book a respiratory assessment.*

    Book Now(opens in a new window)

    *Service available in Ontario only.

    COVID-19 Paxlovid

    Tested positive for COVID-19? You may be eligible to receive Paxlovid, an oral anti-viral medication for COVID-19 patients with mild to moderate symptoms. Book a phone consultation or connect with an online Pharmacist at Vanguard Pharmacy to get assessed and receive a prescription for Paxlovid.
