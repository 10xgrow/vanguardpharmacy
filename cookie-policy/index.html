Cookie Policy

Vanguard Pharmacy Group Ltd

Last Updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is used.

How We Use Cookies

Vanguard Pharmacy uses cookies and similar technologies for several purposes, including:

  • Ensuring the website functions properly
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Analyzing how you use our website to improve our services
  • Delivering relevant advertisements and measuring their effectiveness

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Necessary Cookies (Required)

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the website would not function properly without them.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
vp_cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences 1 year
form_key Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery Session
private_content_version Stores version number to cache private content Session

2. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website and services.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes unique users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttle request rate 1 minute

3. Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_gcl_au Google Ads conversion tracking 3 months
_fbp Facebook pixel for advertising 3 months
IDE DoubleClick - targeted advertising 1 year

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the button below or the "Cookie Settings" button in the bottom left corner of any page on our website.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close the browser

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites you visit.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control the dissemination of these cookies. You should check the respective privacy policies of these third parties for more information about their cookies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for the latest information on our cookie practices.
