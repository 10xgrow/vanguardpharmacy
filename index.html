    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I transfer my prescription? +
    Simply bring your current prescription bottle to our pharmacy, or give us a call. We'll handle the rest with your doctor and insurance.
    Do you offer delivery services? +
    Yes, we offer free prescription delivery within Georgetown. Contact us for more details.
    Can I get a flu shot here? +
    Absolutely! Our pharmacists are certified to administer flu shots and other vaccines. Walk-ins are welcome.
