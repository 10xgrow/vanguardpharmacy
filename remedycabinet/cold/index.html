      Adult Cough, Cold & Flu Remedies

      Relief from fever, sore throat, aches and more.

      Getting cough, cold and flu symptoms under control

      If you’ve caught a cold or the flu, it’s common to have multiple symptoms. Both illnesses can result in coughing, sneezing and a sore throat. Yet fever, aches, and a headache are more commonly associated with a flu virus, while the common cold often results in a wet cough and congestion.

      There are cough, cold and flu remedies that can treat multiple symptoms in one pill and/or syrup. This makes it easier to manage your symptoms. Remember to keep yourself hydrated and comfortable as your body fights off the virus. Check out our selection of Option+ remedies for cough, cold and flu! The option you can trust. Get more for less.       Keep your children comfortable when dealing with a cold or flu

      Cold and flu viruses can spread quickly among children at school and daycare, so keep your Remedy Cabinet stocked with the right medication to treat aches, fevers, coughs and sneezing.

      For flu symptoms in kids, prepare for fever and muscle aches with a children’s medication containing acetaminophen or ibuprofen. Long-lasting relief can help them—and you—sleep through the night. Check out our selection of Option+ remedies for children’s cough, cold and flu relief.
