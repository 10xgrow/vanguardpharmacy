    Vanguard Pharmacy PerfectDose™

    Experience hassle-free medication management with PerfectDose™ packaging. Every dose is precisely organized into easy-to-use, tear-away sections that keep your treatment plan on schedule.

    Managing your prescriptions in 5 simple steps

    Begin with your first dose located at the lower left section.

    Separate an entire row by pressing gently on the blister edge and pulling upward.

    Isolate a single cell by rotating and separating at the seam.

    Apply light pressure along the tear line to reveal the opening.

    Peel back the protective seal and your medicine is ready for use.
