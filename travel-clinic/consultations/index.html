      Taking a Trip Abroad?
      Talk to a Rexall Pharmacist

      Before you embark on your next trip, make sure your health is in check. With Rexall, you can easily access a virtual, travel-knowledgeable pharmacist for a personalized health assessment and travel plan tailored to your destination (a $40 flat fee applies).

      Step 1

      Book Your Travel Health Consultation

      Schedule an appointment and complete our intake form with details about your travel itinerary and health history prior to your appointment.

      Step 2

      Get Your Personal Travel Health Action Plans

      We'll develop a travel health plan with the required vaccines and medications for your destination and offer guidance for managing your health conditions abroad.

      Step 3

      Book Vaccinations, Pick Up Prescriptions, and Travel Essentials

      Your prescriptions and vaccines will be prepared at your preferred Rexall pharmacy.*

      *Travel vaccines may be administered by a Rexall Pharmacist. Prescriptions, medications, and vaccine administration fees are not included in the $40 consultation fee.

      Book Your Consultations

      Frequently Asked Questions

      • In-person appointments are available at Rexall Pharmacist Walk-In Clinics and other select locations. Find the nearest locations near you.

      • During your pre-travel consultation, your pharmacist will provide a personalized travel health assessment tailored to your destination and itinerary. They will inform you about potential health risks and necessary precautions to protect your health during your travels. They will collaborate with prescribers to help you obtain any necessary vaccines or travel medications requiring prescriptions.

      • A Rexall travel health consultation costs $40 per person regardless of the destination youre travelling to. Prescriptions, medications, and vaccine administration fees are not included in the consultation fee.

      • No, travel consultations are not covered by provincial health plans. Some medications and vaccinations may be covered by private or extended health care plans. To learn more, reach out to your health plan provider or employer.

      • You will be asked to complete a travel consult form online during the booking process. This form must be completed before your appointment. A Rexall Pharmacist will review your form and contact you by phone at the time of your appointment.

        To make the most of your consultation, come prepared on the day of your appointment with questions, travel plans (e.g. excursions, activities, accommodations, etc.), and any details about existing health conditions and medications youre taking. You should bring (if available) a list of all the medications you take including non-prescription medications and details of any health benefits.

      • Your travel consultation may take up to 30 minutes.

      • You can receive a travel health consultation from our Virtual Pharmacist team anywhere in Canada. After the consultation, your virtual pharmacist will direct your prescription to one of our 370 Rexall pharmacies nationwide with locations in major cities such as Calgary, Victoria, Edmonton, Saskatoon, Vancouver, Toronto, Surrey, Ottawa, Winnipeg, Hamilton, Kingston, and Regina. You can receive travel advice, vaccines, and medications from your local pharmacist all in one visit, depending on availability and eligibility. Find a nearby pharmacy to get started.
