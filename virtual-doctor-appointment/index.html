    Connect with Ontario Online Doctors through Vanguard Pharmacy

    Vanguard Pharmacy offers you access to convenient, quality healthcare with readily accessible licensed Ontario online doctors through our health partners. You no longer have to wait for weeks to get an appointment or spend hours at a walk-in clinic. Simply choose the health partner available in your province and book a remote doctor’s appointment the same day or the next day.

    Whether you have ongoing health concerns or need immediate care, take control of your health and ask a doctor online by phone, video, or secure messaging. Our health partners can help with medication refills and prescriptions, medical notes and requisitions, medical advice and assessments, mental health support, and more.
