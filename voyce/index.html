    Multilingual Support at Vanguard Pharmacy -
    Communication assistance in 240+ languages available.

    To promote inclusive care and service quality, Vanguard Pharmacy Professionals provide support to patients and visitors facing language challenges or English communication difficulties. Through our partnership with language services covering 240+ languages, we facilitate connections between patients and medical interpreters, ensuring comprehensive pharmacy services including medication counseling and guidance. 

    Interpretation assistance is provided in Mandarin Chinese, Spanish, Ukrainian, Portuguese, Kinyarwanda, Korean, Arabic, French, Hindi, Farsi, Urdu, Turkish, Russian, Cantonese, American Sign Language and numerous additional languages.
